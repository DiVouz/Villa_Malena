Description

Malena is a newly built luxury,spacious villa (130sq meters) with private swimming pool, set in a picturesque,quiet location, less than one kilometre from the traditional Greek village of Glossa (walking distance).

This charming resort, built in a private property of 5 acress, offers guests the opportunity to admire a breathtaking sea view overlooking Aegean and fantastic sunsets.

Free Wi Fi is available.Also you can use the telephone for local calls free of charge.

From infinity style pool you can look over a pine-cloaked hillside,the island of Skiathos and all the way out to the sea.

The villa is situated four kilometres from Loutraki harbour, where you can find a selection of shops,cafeterias and restaurants by the sea.

The detached villa Malena is tastefully furnished and the ground floor consists of a spacious,airy and light lounge with a sattelite tv and a DVD player, a dining room ,a bathroom and a fully equipped open-planned kitchen, which communicates with a covered terrace, where you may have breakfast or meal by staring to the magnificent sea view.

Upstairs the villa boasts comfortable accommodation in two double and one twin air conditioned bedrooms, also features one jakuzzi bathroom.All bedrooms are airy,sunny and share a large veranda,where you can admire the truly panoramic view.

The villa is ideal for couples and families with children, who want to spend their vacations in a luxury,quiet ,totaly private surrounding with magnificent view to the sea and the islands of Skiathos.

For the construction high quality materials have been used such as italian wardrobes and cupbords,double glaze windows etc.

A short drive distance (6km ) is St. John (church on the rock where Mama Mia was filmed ) and also near are the famous beaches Kastani,Milia and Panormos bay.
